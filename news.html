  • Welcome message by Director of the Asia Research Center, Vietnam National University, Hanoi

    • On behalf of the Asia Research Center, Vietnam National University, Hanoi (VNU-ARC), I would like to extend a warm welcome to all participants at “The 6th International Conference on Engineering Mechanics and Automation” (The 6th ICEMA), which is co-organized by the University of Engineering and Technology, Vietnam National University and the Institute of Mechanics, Vietnam Academy of Science and Technology.

    ARC-VNU had the pleasure to provide partly financial support to the organization of ICEMA in 2019 and 2014, which were both successful events. This year, ARC-VNU is happy to continue our co-sponsorship to the 6th ICEMA. We expect that the conference will bring together the latest developments in Engineering Mechanics and Automation and create a valuable forum for all the Vietnamese and international scientists and scholars to share experiences, discuss on various aspects and promote international collaboration in these fields.

    Ladies and Gentlemen,

    Engineering Mechanics and Automation plays a vital role in nearly every industry, from aerospace and automotive to energy, manufacturing, robotics, and biotechnology. In the past decade, Engineering Mechanics and Automation have experienced substantial improvements with a change of focus in terms of scale, span, and applications. Besides, new requirements in terms of design efficiency have become more and more important, pushing to relevant improvements in terms of new methods and advanced approaches. In such circumstance, the organization of the 6th ICEMA has become necessary and of great significance.

    On this special occasion, I would like to extend my sincere gratitude to those who have made efforts to organize this event. I wish the participants useful networking and fruitful exchanges. I also believe that your active participation will be a great contributor to the success of this meaningful Conference.

    Thank you!
